CMDPUBLIC is a collective work spearheaded by Marco Rodriguez in collaboration with friends and colleagues to help provide an engaging and informative outlook on engineering and the arts.

With experience in transforming the methodos of learning and support in a variety of industries, we strive to create an opportunity for everyone to feel that no matter how difficult a task or skill may seem to develop, it is not impossible to understand core concepts and utilize the knowledge picked up along the way and incorporate them into anything without limits.

We hope that you find value in our endevour, and are always welcome to feedback or correspondance.
